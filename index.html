About

I'm Marc Daryll Trinidad, known as Charlie Soniac Spencer — a 4th-year BSIT student at St. Paul University of Surigao. Full-stack developer by practice, debater by challenge, and athlete by discipline.

I code the way I lift — sometimes ugly, always intentional. I'm a vibe coder who uses AI tools to move fast, but I always make sure I understand the problems I'm actually solving. The ideas in my head run faster than my legs ever will — and I'm okay with that.

I'm paradoxical. I stay in rooms that don't always want me, because I know how to grow in uncomfortable soil. I get sad. I wake up neutral. I get back to work. That cycle is the only cheat code I know.
